Pizza Recipe

Pizza on the Grill

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home. The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven. Learn the tricks for making and grilling pizza from scratch with this recipe. Feel free to use your favorite toppings, but keep in mind not to overload the pizza.

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course, you can use whatever toppings you like!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 pinch white sugar
  • 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2: Make dough: Pour warm water into a large bowl
  3. Step 3: Place dough in a well-oiled bowl and cover with a damp cloth.
  4. Step 4: Top with 1/2 of each of the following: tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, olives, red peppers, cheese, and basil.
