Curry recipe

A wonderful Indian curry dish that is easy and quick to make. Using the base recipe, you can make several variations of this delicious comfort food.

Garnish with cilantro and serve over Indian curry rice. If the paneer cheese can't be found, potatoes or chicken can be used.

Ingredients

  • Oilive oil (1 cup)
  • Onion, chopped (1)
  • Ginger, minced (1 tspn)
  • Pepper, minced (2)
  • Red chilli powder (1 tspn)
  • Ground cumin (1 tspn)
  • Ground corriander (1 tspn)
