Stir Fry recipe

Tasty, quick, simple, and easy to cook vegan stir-fry.

Sizzle up one of our fragrant stir-fry recipes for a speedy midweek meal. Season your wok with everything from Singapore noodles and chicken chow mein to pad Thai and special fried rice.

Make your own easy stir-fry sauce at home with storecupboard ingredients and combine with chicken, veg and cashews for a filling dinner.

Ingredients

  • Package dried soba noodles (8ou)
  • Oil (1 tbsp)
  • Onion
  • Garlic, finely chopped (2)
  • Mushrooms (1 cup)
  • Eggplant, chopped (1 cup)
  • Bok choy, chopped (6 leaves)
  • Soy sauce, (2 tbsp)
  • Teriyaki sauce, (1 tspn)
  • Ground black pepper
  • Sesame oil, (1 tspn)
  • Green onion, finely chopped (1)

Steps

  1. Step 1: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook soba in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until noodles are tender yet firm to the bite, 5 to 6 minutes. Drain.
  2. Step 2: Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and stir-fry for 1 minute. Toss in mushrooms and eggplant; cook for 2 minutes more. Add cooked soba noodles, bok choy, soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, and pepper. Cook until bok choy is tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Step 3: Sprinkle sesame oil and green onion over vegetables and serve.
